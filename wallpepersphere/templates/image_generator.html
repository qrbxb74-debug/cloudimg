How It Works

Your text prompt is sent to Google's powerful Imagen-3 model on Vertex AI. The AI interprets your words to create a brand new image. This process typically takes 5-15 seconds.

Prompting Tips

  • Be specific: "A photorealistic image of a red sports car" is better than "a car".
  • Add style: Use words like "cyberpunk", "fantasy", "cinematic", "watercolor".
  • Set the scene: Mention lighting like "golden hour" or "moody neon lights".
